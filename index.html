The Pipeline

Four stages. Total isolation.

No analyst sees another's work until synthesis. Bias has nowhere to travel.

Our Mission

The reproducibility crisis is a scientific emergency.

Over 50% of studies in psychology fail independent replication. In biomedicine, the figure is higher. Authors routinely over-state certainty, under-report assumptions, and omit data that a reproducer would need.

OpenParasite deploys isolated AI analysts to do what independent replication cannot — audit every paper, at scale, immediately after publication.

Science advances by standing on what came before.

But if the foundation is hollow, the whole structure falls. We verify the foundation.

Peer review is a filter with holes.

We're the net underneath. Not to punish researchers — to help science trust itself again.

Open source and free, always.

Scientific infrastructure should be public. OpenParasite is built in the open and free to use for any researcher.
